About Me & My Expertise

My Story

I'm Nikita Shrestha, a passionate graphic designer with 3+ years of experience creating visually compelling designs that communicate brand stories effectively.

My journey in design began during my internship at Codynn Company, where I discovered my love for creating brand identities that resonate with audiences. Since then, I've worked with clients across industries to develop memorable visual experiences.

I believe good design should be both beautiful and functional, creating emotional connections while serving its purpose. My approach combines strategic thinking with artistic expression to deliver designs that stand out.
